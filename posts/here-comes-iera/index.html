1. Hello IERA!

After lots lots of days of efforts, IERA is founded, improved, and ready to see you! IERA is short for “Industrial Engineering ERA”, a non-profit website and platform for introducing, spreading, and enhancing the knowledge of Industrial Engineering to people over the world. IERA also serves as a central hub to connect audiences of industrial scientists, academic researchers, engineers, students and those who are interested in IE to share ideas, knowledge, and experience.

IERA is composed by a blog system and a forum. The blog will publish high-quality articles in IE field, and the forum is a place for every user to have discussions. For more information, please refer to the website http://iera.name/

2. I need your help!

If you are interested in sharing your own articles with others, editing others’ articles, maintaining a website, or in charge of a forum, please contact me! You may also register the website and participate in our blog system and the forum discussions. Happiness always lies in your sharing with others!

Welcome, and have fun!
